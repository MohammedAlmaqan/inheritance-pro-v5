# Privacy Policy - Inheritance Pro v5.0

**Effective Date**: February 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Inheritance Pro is a professional Islamic inheritance calculator developed to assist users with inheritance calculations according to Islamic fiqh principles. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### What We DO Collect

- **Device Information**: 
  - Device model, OS version, architecture
  - App version and build number
  - Crash reports (if enabled)

- **Usage Analytics** (Optional):
  - Features used
  - Calculation frequency
  - Session duration
  - Error logs

### What We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal identification data (names, ID numbers, etc.)
- ❌ Inheritance calculation details
- ❌ Estate values or amounts
- ❌ Heir information
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Financial transaction data
- ❌ Browsing behavior
- ❌ Biometric data

## 3. Data Processing

### On-Device Processing

**All calculations are performed locally on your device:**

- Estate calculations
- Heir distribution
- Fiqh calculations
- Result generation

✅ **Data stays on your device** - Nothing is sent to servers

### Optional Cloud Features

If we add cloud sync in future versions:
- Users can opt-in explicitly
- Data encryption end-to-end
- Clear consent required
- Users can delete all data anytime
